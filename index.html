Old Court is a unique location for your wedding and reception. Set on the shores of Strangford Lough in a private estate with a 17th century Chapel, restored stables, a yurt-inspired Marquee and several acres of natural gardens along the Shore.

The opportunities for fabulous photos are quite breathtaking. As we only have One wedding per week, the venue will be Exclusively yours, with the opportunity to Customise the space prior to the big day, if you so wish, so that you can feel completely Relaxed, with everything as you would like it, ready to enjoy a Memorable day and evening.
